Domiciliary Accounts

This allows you to maintain accounts in foreign currencies (USD, GBP and EUR). Transactions (deposits & withdrawals) are carried out in the same currency in which the account is denominated.

Documenation

  • Completed Account Opening Forms
  • Valid means of Identification
  • One recent passport sized photograph
  • Documentary Evidence of Address in Nigeria
  • One satisfactory reference
  • CERPAC- Combined Expatriate Residence Permit and Alien Card (West African nationals excluded)
