ALT BIZ FACILITY

ALT BIZ facility is a product designed to provide quick funds for small businesses to augment working capital and for asset (equipment) acquisition.

Benefits includes;

  • Up to N5M financing limit per business!
  • No guarantors required!
  • Monthly, 90 Days, 180 Days Repayment Cycles
  • Funds are solely for the purchase of raw materials, stock or equipments

    • Ratings includes;

  • Commitment Deposit 20% of facility amount
  • Insurance cost 2% of facility amount p.a
  • Markup rate 14% flat p.a

    • Requirements

  • 1 month relationship with SAF corporate
  • Application letter containing the desired facility amount and tenor
  • AltBiz application form duly completed
  • AltBiz Profiler
  • 12 months’ financial statement from SAF and/or other banks
  • Business registration documents from the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC)
  • Proof of 2 years’ business existence (any document that shows the business has existed for 2 years e.g. invoice receipts, utility bill etc.)
  • Proof of business location (Recent utility bills, rent receipts, Lease agreements)
  • A Proforma’s invoice addressed to SAF with details of financed asset
  • Valid means of identification
  • Favorable credit reports on business and promoter
  • A commitment deposit – 20% of facility amount should be in SAF corporate account
  • 2% per annum cost of insurance on financed asset should be in the SAF corporate account
