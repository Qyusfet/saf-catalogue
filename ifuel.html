iFuel Solution

iFuel solution is a self-service platform that gives customers the opportunity to request for diesel supply at a competitive price from one of the registered vendors on the platform.

iFuel is a retail marketplace that allows diesel suppliers to connect to its end-users (mostly institutions, individuals and industries) while the Bank provides payment capability and credit finance (where necessary) to aid transactions.

BENEFITS OF IFUEL SOLUTION TO CUSTOMERS

  • Diesel purchase at a lower cost to customers
  • Quality product guaranteed because the vendors are pre-registered
  • Faster TAT
  • Pricing Transparency (No hidden charges)
  • Product finance option is available subject to Terms & Conditions.
  • Transparent pricing for all diesel end users and wholesalers
  • Provide a guaranteed source of high-quality diesel

BENEFITS OF IFUEL SOLUTION TO VENDORS

  • Increased volume of sales and customer reach
  • Digital platform to connect customers directly to vendors
  • Elimination of agents (middlemen in the supply chain) will lead to Increased revenue for the vendors
  • Faster payment for delivered product
  • Request and delivery are digitally managed to increase vendors productivity.
