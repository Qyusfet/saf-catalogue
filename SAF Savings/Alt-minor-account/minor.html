SAF MINOR ACCOUNT

The SAF minor account is a profit sharing account designed for minors (children) guided by the principles of Islamic commercial jurisprudence

It is designed to encourage the development of a strong saving culture amongst children. It may be operated by the child (with parental supervision) or the parents with a view towards a long-term investment horizon.

This account operates under the Mudarabah contract where the Bank acts as the manager of funds and depositors are entitled to profit share on the amount deposited based on the agreed ratio.

Benefits

  • Targeted towards cultivating an early savings culture among children
  • Opportunity to save and enjoy Non-interest banking facilities
  • Deposits can be made through cash, cheques and online transfers.
  • Access to SMS and email alerts

Requirements

  • Complete an account opening application form or fill online account form
  • Valid means of identification
  • One recent passport sized Photograph
