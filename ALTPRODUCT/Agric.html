Agriculture Finance

Alternative Finance supports operators in the agricultural sector
through a special mode of financing. It is available to co-operatives
and registered farmers who require funds to cultivate and deliver their
crops.

Quality and quantity are also expected to be specified from inception.
This is otherwise known as a salam contract.

Requirements

  • Alternative Finance corporate accounts. Click here to open account .
  • Evidence of ready market to sell the commodity (off-takers)
  • Clear evidence of difference between quality and quantity of commodities
  • Date of delivery
  • Place of delivery
  • Collateral
  • Business Plan
  • Cash Flow projection
