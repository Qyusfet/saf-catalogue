Hajj Savings

The Pilgrimage savings account is a profit sharing account guided by the principles of Non-interest banking concepts.

The product makes the pilgrimage dream a reality as prospective pilgrims conveniently save towards fufilling their religious obligatory rights

This account operates under the Mudarabah contract where the Bank acts as the manager of funds and depositors are entitled to profit share on the amount deposited based on the agreed ratio.

Benefits

  • Opportunity to earn returns in a manner compliant with Non-interest banking principles
  • Free access to internet banking facilities and and other e-banking solutions
  • Availability of customized pilgrimage Debit Cards for pilgrimage
  • Multiple investment permissible and can run concurrently
  • Access to interest free personal loan

Requirements

  • Complete a pilgrimage savings account opening form
  • Valid means of identification
  • One recent passport sized Photograph
