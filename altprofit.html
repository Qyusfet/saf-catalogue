Profit Share Investment

These accounts will run on the Sterling Alternative banking of mudarabah which is a profit sharing partnership agreement between the provider of funds(depositors) and Investment manager(the Bank).


Funds shall be used mostly for trade transactions involving permissible items and commodities. The profits are shared according to pre-agreed ratios between the customer and the Bank. Losses suffered not on account of negligence or breach of Investment mandate by the Bank shall be borne by the depositors/accountholders.


This product is available to;

  • Customers with a desire to make lucrative long term Investment
  • High Net worth individuals
  • Financial institutions
  • Members of groups, associations/cooperatives and corporate enterprises

General Requirements

  • Opening Balance( Minimum deposit of N500,000 )
  • Daily Minimum Balance(Not applicable)
  • MinimumTenure(1 year)
  • Expected Profit Share(Variable with a benchmark)
