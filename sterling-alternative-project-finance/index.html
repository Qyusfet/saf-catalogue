Sterling Alternative Project Finance

Sterling Alternative Project Finance operates based on the Non-Interest Islamic banking principle of lstisna' in which the Bank undertakes to enter to produce or manufacture certain asset or project for a client based on agreed specification at a determined price and for future/fixed date of delivery.

This product is available to;

  • Customers seeking to finance developmental projects in real estate and manufacturing
  • Real estate companies
  • Manufacturers
  • Financial institutions
  • Members of groups, associations or cooperatives
  • Corporate enterprises
  • Government parastatals

    • General Requirements for self-employed individual/professional

  • Profile of individuals and details of business transactions and experience in the line of business
  • 12 months statement of account from existing bankers
  • Details of borrowings with other banks and security

    • General Requirements for Corporate entities

  • Structure of existing business and track record in the line of business
  • Management and company profile
  • 12 months statement of account from existing bankers
  • Audited financial statement for 3 years and latest management account

    • Maximum Credit - 80% of the value of goods

    Maximum Tenure - Up to 48 months

    Pricing - On rental basis which would be disclosed and agreed with the client

    Other Requirements - Insurance of the financed asset under the Takaful (insurance) policy
